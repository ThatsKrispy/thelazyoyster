We use essential cookies for website functionality and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You may decline non-essential cookies via our cookie consent banner. Refer to your browser settings to manage cookies at any time.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected for or as required by law. Contact us to request deletion of your data.

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at StayLazy@TheLazyOyster.com. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
