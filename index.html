The Lazy Oyster

Welcome to The Lazy Oyster Miami's first traveling oyster bar. We deliver the freshest oysters straight to your events or your doorstep.

Founded by lifelong friends Alex Gilardi and Brandon Mesa, with over a decade of hospitality experience between them, The Lazy Oyster brings a custom catering experience to every celebration big or small.

From intimate gatherings to grand events, pop-up raw bars to convenient delivery, our expert team makes every detail flawless and every bite unforgettable.
