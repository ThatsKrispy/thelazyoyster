Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

The Lazy Oyster is Miami’s premier oyster delivery and raw bar catering service. From Wednesday to Sunday, you can have the freshest oysters delivered straight to your doorstep. Our menu is updated weekly on our Instagram and website, offering a rotating selection of the finest oysters. You can mix and match all varieties, with a minimum order of 2 dozen. Choose between delivery or warehouse pickup. Orders can be placed online or via Instagram DM, and our team will confirm your order. We offer raw oysters and chargrilled oyster kits for you to prepare at home.
Absolutely! We offer unshucked oysters at a discounted price, perfect for those who enjoy the shucking experience themselves.
We recommend ordering at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee availability. Same-day delivery may be available depending on our schedule — reach out via Instagram DM or call us to check.
Follow us on Instagram at @TheLazyOyster for live updates on our pop-up locations, event appearances, and weekly menus.
We source our oysters from trusted, sustainable farms and receive fresh deliveries multiple times per week. Shucked oysters are prepared the same day of your order. Unshucked oysters are packed with ice and delivered in insulated containers to keep them at the perfect temperature.
You can order directly at orderthelazyoyster.com or by reaching out to us via Instagram DM or phone at (305) 905-0257.
Shucked oysters arrive on an iced aluminum tray with mignonette sauce, cocktail sauce, lemon wedges, and the top shells. Unshucked oysters come in a breathable mesh bag or box, properly iced to maintain freshness throughout delivery.
You can pre-order up to 2 weeks in advance. We recommend booking ahead for weekends and holidays to guarantee availability. The earlier, the better!
Shucked oysters are best consumed the same day. Unshucked oysters can be stored in your refrigerator for 3–5 days, cup side down on a flat tray covered with a damp cloth or newspaper. Never store them on ice or in standing water.
We’re Miami’s original traveling oyster bar, founded in 2020 with a mission to make fresh, high-quality oysters accessible to everyone. Our team brings over a decade of hospitality experience, a commitment to freshness, and a passion for creating memorable experiences.
Oysters are available year-round! While flavor profiles can vary slightly by season (slightly creamier in winter, brinier in summer), our sourcing ensures great-tasting oysters every time you order.
