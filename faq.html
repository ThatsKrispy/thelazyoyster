The Lazy Oyster is Miami’s premier oyster delivery and raw bar catering service. From Wednesday to Sunday, you can have the freshest oysters delivered straight to your doorstep. We source our oysters from trusted, sustainable farms and receive fresh deliveries multiple times per week. Shucked oysters are prepared the same day of your order. Unshucked oysters are packed with ice in insulated containers to maintain perfect temperature.

Shucked oysters arrive on an iced aluminum tray with mignonette sauce, cocktail sauce, lemon wedges, and the top shells. Unshucked oysters come properly iced in breathable packaging to maintain freshness throughout delivery.
You can pre-order up to 2 weeks in advance. We recommend booking ahead for weekends and holidays to guarantee availability. The earlier, the better!
Shucked oysters are best consumed the same day. Unshucked oysters can be stored in your refrigerator for 3–5 days, cup side down on a flat tray covered with a damp cloth. Never store them on ice or in standing water.
