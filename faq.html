We've Got Answers

We offer delivery and pickup Wednesday through Sunday. Orders must be placed at least 24 hours in advance. Check our ordering platform at orderthelazyoyster.com for available time slots in your area.

We serve Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. Delivery availability and fees vary by zone. Check the ordering platform to confirm delivery to your specific address before placing your order.

Extremely fresh. Our oysters are sourced from certified waters and handled with strict cold-chain care. Shucked oysters are prepared the same day as delivery and packed on ice. We do not sell oysters that are more than one day old.

Yes! Our unshucked bulk oysters are perfect for DIY home events. We also offer larger quantity shucked orders — just reach out to us directly at StayLazy@TheLazyOyster.com or call (305) 905-0257 for large home-event orders and pricing.

Yes, pickup is available from our Miami location. When you place an order on our ordering platform, you'll have the option to select pickup. You'll receive a confirmation with the exact pickup location and instructions.

We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for most events. For larger events (100+ guests) or peak season dates (holidays, spring/fall weekends), 6–8 weeks is strongly recommended. Reach out early — dates fill quickly.

All catering packages include setup and breakdown, a professional shucker, oysters on ice with sauces and garnishes, and serving ware. Premium packages add lobster rolls, ceviche, custom presentation, and a dedicated event coordinator. Exact inclusions vary by package — reach out and we'll tailor a quote to your event.

We cater all types of events — weddings, corporate gatherings, birthday parties, bridal and baby showers, holiday parties, pop-up markets, brewery events, and private backyard parties. If you have guests and want oysters, we're in.

Yes! We serve all of South Florida including Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. For events outside of these areas, reach out to us directly at StayLazy@TheLazyOyster.com — we'll do our best to accommodate.

After submitting your catering inquiry, one of our team members will contact you to confirm details and send a formal quote. A deposit is required to secure your date. We accept major credit cards, Venmo, and Zelle. Full payment is due before or on the event date.

Our setup involves live shellfish handling. If you or a guest has a serious shellfish allergy, please consult your allergist. We take every precaution but cannot guarantee a shellfish-free environment during our events. Please let us know about any dietary concerns when booking.
