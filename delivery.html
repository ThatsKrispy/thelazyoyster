Convenient Pickup & Delivery

Fresh Oysters,
Your Way.

Order online for delivery to your door or pickup at our Miami location. Available Wednesday through Sunday.

Step 1

Place Your Order

Select from our premium oyster options, including fresh oysters (shucked or unshucked) and mouthwatering lobster rolls. Add your favorite sauces and extras to customize your order.

Checkout the Menu →
Step 2

Schedule Your Delivery or Pickup

Decide whether you’d like your seafood delivered to your doorstep or if you’d prefer to pick it up yourself. During checkout, choose the date and time that works best for you.

Step 3

Expert Preparation

Shucked Oysters: Our skilled shuckers prepare your oysters just before delivery or pickup. Served on an iced aluminum tray with sauces, lemon wedges, and the top shells to keep the oyster liquor intact.

Unshucked Oysters: Prefer to shuck them yourself? Your unshucked oysters will be packed fresh, ready for you to enjoy on your schedule.

Step 4

Savor the Experience

Whether it’s a cozy night in or a special gathering, your oysters and lobster rolls arrive fresh, delicious, and ready to enjoy. From expertly shucked oysters to indulgent lobster rolls, we bring the taste of the sea to you!
