How It Works

Fresh Oysters in 4 Easy Steps

Step 1

Place Your Order

Select from our premium oyster options, including fresh oysters (shucked or unshucked) and our famous Miami lobster rolls. Add your favorite sauces and extras to customize your order.

Checkout the Menu
Step 2

Schedule Delivery or Pickup

Decide whether you'd like your seafood delivered to your doorstep or prefer to pick it up yourself. During checkout, choose the date and time that works best for you. We deliver Wednesday through Sunday.

Step 3

Expert Preparation

Shucked Oysters: Our skilled shuckers will prepare your oysters just before delivery or pickup. They'll be served on an iced aluminum tray with sauces, lemon wedges, and the top shells to keep the oyster liquor intact.

Unshucked Oysters: Prefer to shuck them yourself? Your unshucked oysters will be packed fresh, ready for you to enjoy on your schedule.

Step 4

Savor the Experience

Whether it's a cozy night in or a special gathering, your oysters and lobster rolls arrive fresh, delicious, and ready to enjoy. From expertly shucked oysters to indulgent lobster rolls, we bring the taste of the sea to you!
