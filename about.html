Our Story

About The Lazy Oyster

Meet the Founders

Alex & Brandon

The Lazy Oyster was born in 2020 from a shared passion between lifelong friends Alex Gilardi and Brandon Mesa. With over a decade of combined hospitality experience, they saw an opportunity to bring something Miami had never seen: a traveling oyster bar that delivers the raw bar experience wherever you need it.

What started as Miami's first oyster delivery service quickly evolved into a full catering operation — live shucking at events, pop-up raw bars across South Florida, and an ever-growing menu of fresh seafood favorites.
