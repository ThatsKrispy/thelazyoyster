Our Story

How It Started

"If you can laugh together, you can work together."

The Lazy Oyster started in 2020 as Miami's first oyster delivery service, quickly evolving into a full raw bar catering experience serving all three South Florida counties.

Founded by lifelong friends Alex Gilardi and Brandon Mesa, our mission is to bring high-quality, responsibly sourced oysters to your events and pop-ups around the city.

With 10+ years combined hospitality experience, Alex and Brandon treat every order and event — from 10 guests to 500 — with the same passion and care.
