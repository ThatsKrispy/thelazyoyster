Meet the Shuckers

Alex & Brandon

The dynamic duo behind The Lazy Oyster

The Lazy Oyster started in 2020 as Miami’s first oyster delivery service, quickly evolving into a full raw bar catering experience that shows up anywhere in South Florida.

Founded by lifelong friends Alex Gilardi and Brandon Mesa, our mission is simple: bring high-quality, responsibly sourced oysters to your events and pop-ups around the city.

With over 10 years of combined hospitality experience between them, Alex and Brandon believe that oysters are a symbol of celebration and joy. After all — who can be sad while eating an oyster?
