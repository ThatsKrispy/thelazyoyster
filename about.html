Miami's First Traveling Oyster Bar

If you can laugh together, you can work together.

The Lazy Oyster started in 2020 as Miami’s first oyster delivery service, quickly evolving into a full raw bar catering experience.

Founded by lifelong friends Alex Gilardi and Brandon Mesa, our mission is to bring high-quality, responsibly sourced oysters to your events and pop-ups around the city.

Meet the Shuckers

Driven by a passion for shucking and serving.

With over 10 years of experience in the hospitality industry, Alex brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise. Brandon, also from a hospitality background, shares a deep commitment to exceptional service.

They believe that oysters are a symbol of celebration and joy. After all, who can be sad while eating an oyster? Oysters are enjoyed during special moments, marking the start of something great.

At The Lazy Oyster, we believe that every time you enjoy our oysters, you’re on the brink of a memorable experience. We love shucking oysters for you because they signify the beginning of your great days and nights.

Miami’s Freshest Ceviche Experience

At The Lazy Oyster, we believe ceviche is more than just a dish — it’s a celebration of freshness and flavor. Our signature recipe blends hand cut seafood, crisp vegetables, and bright citrus to capture the essence of Miami in every bite.

Whether you’re hosting guests or treating yourself, our ceviche brings a burst of coastal energy and elegance to any occasion.

Our Commitment

Freshness, Quality, and Sustainability

At The Lazy Oyster, we are committed to delivering the freshest oysters, sourced responsibly from local farms.

Our meticulous attention to quality and sustainability ensures that every oyster we serve is a testament to our dedication. Whether at intimate gatherings or large events, we strive to create memorable experiences, one oyster at a time.
