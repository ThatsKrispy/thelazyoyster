Accessibility Statement

The Lazy Oyster is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

Measures We've Taken

  • Keyboard navigation support throughout the site
  • Skip navigation link at the top of every page
  • Descriptive alt text on all meaningful images
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios across text and backgrounds
  • ARIA labels and roles on interactive elements
  • Responsive design across all screen sizes and devices
  • Respects prefers-reduced-motion for users who prefer less animation
  • Focus indicators visible on all interactive elements

Known Limitations

Some third-party content (including our ordering platform at orderthelazyoyster.com) may not fully conform to accessibility standards. We are not responsible for the accessibility of third-party services.
